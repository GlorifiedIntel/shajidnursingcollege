Our Story

About Us

At Shajid Royal College of Nursing and Midwifery, we are committed to shaping compassionate, skilled, and knowledgeable healthcare professionals who are prepared to meet the evolving needs of the healthcare industry. Established with a vision to empower the next generation of nurses and midwives, our college stands as a beacon of excellence in nursing and midwifery education.

We offer a range of accredited programs designed to combine rigorous academic training with practical clinical experience. Mission Statement

At Shajid Royal College of Nursing and Midwifery, our mission is to educate, inspire, and empower compassionate healthcare professionals through excellence in nursing and midwifery education, clinical practice, and community service. We are committed to producing highly skilled, ethically grounded, and culturally sensitive graduates who will contribute to improving healthcare outcomes, particularly in underserved and rural communities. Guided by integrity, innovation, and a passion for care, we strive to shape leaders who advance health and uphold the dignity of every human life. Shajid Royal College Mission Statement

Our Aims

The aims of Shajid Royal College of Nursing and Midwifery are to provide high-quality, evidence-based education that prepares students to become competent, compassionate, and ethical healthcare professionals. The college is committed to developing skilled nurses and midwives who can address the diverse health needs of individuals, families, and communities, particularly in underserved areas. It seeks to promote excellence in clinical practice, encourage lifelong learning, and foster leadership and innovation in healthcare. Through strong community engagement, research, and collaboration with health institutions, the college aims to contribute to the improvement of maternal, child, and public health while upholding the highest standards of professional integrity and human dignity.

Why Shajid Royal College

Shajid Royal College of Nursing and Midwifery stands out as a center of excellence dedicated to nurturing the next generation of healthcare professionals. With a strong focus on academic rigor, practical training, and compassionate care, the college provides a supportive learning environment that blends modern medical knowledge with ethical and culturally sensitive practice. Our experienced faculty, well-equipped clinical facilities, and community-focused approach ensure that students graduate not only with technical competence but also with a heart for service. Whether you're passionate about making a difference in maternal and child health or committed to advancing nursing practice, Shajid College is the ideal place to begin your journey toward a fulfilling and impactful career in healthcare.

Strategic Objectives of Shajid Royal College

Shajid Royal College of Nursing and Midwifery is committed to achieving strategic objectives that drive academic excellence, professional development, and community impact. These objectives include enhancing the quality of nursing and midwifery education through continuous curriculum review and faculty development; expanding access to state-of-the-art learning resources and clinical training facilities; and fostering a culture of research and innovation in healthcare. The college also aims to strengthen partnerships with healthcare institutions and regulatory bodies to ensure relevance and accreditation, promote community-based healthcare initiatives that address local and national health challenges, and ensure the holistic development of students by instilling leadership, ethical values, and a strong sense of service. Through these strategic goals, the college strives to position itself as a leading institution in nursing and midwifery education within the region and beyond.
