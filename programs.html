Shajid College Programs Overview

Shajid College of Nursing and Midwifery is dedicated to training compassionate, skilled, and competent healthcare professionals who are committed to improving community health. Our programs in Nursing and Midwifery combine rigorous academic instruction with hands-on clinical experience, preparing students to meet the evolving challenges of modern healthcare. With a strong emphasis on ethical practice, patient-centered care, and professional development, Shajid College equips graduates with the knowledge and confidence to excel in hospitals, clinics, and community health settings both locally and globally.
