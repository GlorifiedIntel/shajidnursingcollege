Nursing Applicants

Thank you for your interest in the Nursing program at Shajid College of Nursing. Our program is designed for individuals passionate about making a difference in healthcare through compassionate, evidence-based nursing practice. We offer a comprehensive curriculum that blends classroom instruction with hands-on clinical experience, equipping students with the skills needed to excel in diverse healthcare settings. Applicants should demonstrate a strong academic background, a commitment to patient care, and a desire to grow professionally in the nursing field. Join us to begin a rewarding journey toward becoming a trusted and skilled nurse. Apply now to take the first step in your healthcare career.

Midwifery Applicants

Thank you for your interest in the Midwifery program at Shajid College of Nursing and Midwifery. Our program is designed to equip aspiring midwives with the knowledge, clinical skills, and compassionate care practices needed to support women and families throughout pregnancy, childbirth, and postpartum. With a strong foundation in evidence-based healthcare, hands-on training in modern clinical settings, and guidance from experienced faculty, students are prepared to become confident, competent, and ethical midwifery professionals. We welcome dedicated applicants who are passionate about maternal and newborn health and ready to make a meaningful impact in the healthcare field.
