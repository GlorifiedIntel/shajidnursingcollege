Scholarships & Financial Assistance

To promote academic excellence and equal opportunity, Shajid Royal College offers merit-based and need-based financial support:

Types of Aid Available

  1. Merit Scholarships (based on entrance exam performance)
  2. Need-Based Bursaries (for students facing financial hardship)
  3. Sibling Discounts (for families with more than one enrolled student)
  4. Outstanding Performance Awards (for top-performing students annually)

How to Apply: Submit a Financial Aid Application Form to the Office of Student Affairs with required documentation during the first 2 weeks of each academic year.

External Sponsorships & Partnerships

We actively collaborate with government agencies, NGOs, and health sector sponsors to support students. Past sponsors include:

  1. State Government Nursing Scholarships
  2. Private Hospital Support Programs
  3. International Health Education Foundations

If you have a sponsor, please submit a Letter of Sponsorship at the time of registration.
